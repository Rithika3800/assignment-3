SONY WH-1000XM5

The Sony WH-1000XM5 headphones are high-end wireless headphones known for their exceptional noise-cancellation technology, which effectively blocks out external sounds for an immersive listening experience. They are designed with comfort in mind, featuring plush ear cushions and a lightweight build, making them suitable for extended wear. The headphones also deliver impressive sound quality with clear highs, rich mids, and deep bass, enhancing the enjoyment of music, movies, and more. Additionally, they come equipped with advanced features like Adaptive Sound Control, which automatically adjusts the noise cancellation based on your activity and environment, ensuring optimal performance in any situation. With a long battery life, they are perfect for travel or daily use, making them a popular choice among discerning users in the premium headphone market.

With two processors controlling eight microphones, Auto NC Optimizer for automatically optimizing noise cancelling based on your wearing conditions and environment, and a specially designed driver unit, WH-1000XM5 headphones with industry-leading noise cancelling1 rewrite the rules for distraction-free listening and call clarity.
